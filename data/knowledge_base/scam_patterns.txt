RISK KNOWLEDGE BASE: COMMON SCAM PATTERNS
-----------------------------------------
1. HONEYPOT SCAM:
   - Definition: A token smart contract that allows users to buy but prevents them from selling.
   - Indicators:
     * "100% Sell Tax" or extremely high sell fees (e.g., >50%).
     * Code contains functions like "disableTrading()" or "blacklist()".
     * Only the creator wallet can sell.

2. RUG PULL (Liquidity Drain):
   - Definition: Developers withdraw all liquidity from the DEX (Decentralized Exchange), making the token worthless.
   - Indicators:
     * Liquidity is NOT locked (or locked for a very short time, e.g., < 1 month).
     * One wallet holds >50% of the total supply.
     * The developers are completely anonymous with no social reputation.

3. FAKE AIRDROP / PHISHING:
   - Definition: Users are promised free tokens but must connect their wallet to a malicious site.
   - Indicators:
     * Website asks for "Seed Phrase" or "Private Key" (IMMEDIATE RED FLAG).
     * Unrealistic promises (e.g., "Claim 1000 ETH for free").
